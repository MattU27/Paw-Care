About PawCare Connect

Learn more about our mission, values, and the team behind PawCare Connect

Our Story

Founded in 2020, PawCare Connect was born from a simple idea: to create a seamless experience for pet owners by combining premium pet supplies with accessible veterinary care.

What started as a small local operation has grown into a trusted platform serving thousands of pet owners nationwide. Our founder, Emma Parker, a lifelong animal lover and former veterinary technician, recognized the need for a more integrated approach to pet care.

"I wanted to create a one-stop solution where pet parents could find everything they need for their furry family members," says Emma. "From nutritious food to expert medical care, we're dedicated to supporting the full spectrum of pet wellness."

Our Mission

Pet Wellbeing First
