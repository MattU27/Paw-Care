Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule a vet appointment?

You can schedule a vet appointment through our "Book Vet Care" page, by calling our office directly at (555) 987-6543, or by sending an email to appointments@pawcareconnect.com with your preferred date and time.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase for most products in their original packaging. Food and treats can only be returned if unopened. Please contact our customer service team for specific return instructions.

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes! We offer standard shipping nationwide, with free shipping on orders over $50. For customers in the San Francisco Bay Area, we also offer same-day delivery for orders placed before 2 PM.
